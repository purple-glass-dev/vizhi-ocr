Vizhi OCR

VizhiOCR lives in your MacOS menu bar. Press a shortcut, draw a box over any part of your screen, and get complex text, messy handwriting, and mathematical equations back as clean markdown or tables in your clipboard (using SOTA AI models).
It runs completely offline on your Mac, so your screenshots never leave your machine. And yes! you can also a import pdf files/images from your computer and preview/save the conversion to a markdown file

How it works

Get text and tables from your screen in three easy steps.

01

Screen Capture/Upload

Press your shortcut key and select a region on your screen, or drop a PDF document into the app.

02

Local Scan

The app reads the image directly on your computer's chip using local AI or the built-in system scanner.

03

Preview and Correct

Check the text next to the image and make quick edits if you want

04

Paste or Save to markdown

Copy the final result to clipboard and paste it wherever needed OR Save the structured output as markdown
